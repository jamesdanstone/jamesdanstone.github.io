Wee spring fern

Inspired during forest dog walks, this personal project explored the unfurling leaves of a fern in the springtime. I enjoyed creating the contrast of this beautiful awakening into spring and the unfortunate shower that follows. You are also treated to some of my musical talents, for which I can only apologise!
