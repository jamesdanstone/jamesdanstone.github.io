Wonderlab

Wonderlab is a vibrant, hands-on gallery at the Railway Museum where children can discover how railways work and why they matter. This social campaign combined footage and collage style animation to create an out-of-this-world look at how the different activities explore science and engineering.

Process

We weren't able to shoot anything on green screen for these films, so all the footage is cutouts of kids playing at Wonderlab with 3D and 2d assets added to give that exagerrated look and bring the real world examples to life!
