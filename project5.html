Storm cybercare

Protecting businesses from all things cyber! Storm wanted to advertise the range of services they provide and demonstrate how important it is to be protected.

Process

To acheive the complex 3d grid movements involved in the video I experimented with effectors to move and rotate tiles. This created smoother and more organic motion, yet still with the ability to override using keyframes when greater control was required. This animation technique required more time to rig and set up each scene but was then much more flexible when it came to adjustments and finessing.
