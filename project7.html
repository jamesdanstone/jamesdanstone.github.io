Forge Valley Roasters

This brand film for a speciality coffee producer based in the North York Moors celebrated the passion and craft shown by owners Chris and Tash.


The film was based entirely around an unscripted interview with them, and allowed their narrative and story to take the lead, using a blend of 2D, 3D and collage style graphics to bring out the flavour. It was great to experiment, finding ways to encapsulate the different aspects of their business while the couples love for coffee percolated throughout.
