About Me

Let’s get to know each other a bit better, I'll go first.


When I'm not smoothing curves and tweaking transitions you can often find me and my family walking the dog in the Yorkshire Dales, often finding inspiration in the process. Originally I set out in life to become a sports psychologist, studying in Liverpool for three years and although the love for sports has remained I found a different calling…


After using the Adobe suite to create some videos for a paper company (alas not Dunder Mifflin, Scranton, PA) and being completely hooked on the content cuts and keyframes. I started making videos for friends' businesses and local events, luckily through word of mouth the animation requests kept coming. Fast forward a few years and countless freelance projects later, I joined Hewitt & Walker, a fantastic little production company based in York. During 3 years there I gained invaluable experience, refining techniques, tidying workflows and absorbing all the knowledge I could from my fellow creatives.


Now, armed with the secrets of motion design, a killer workflow and the confidence to create something special from any brief I want to work with you.
